MEDITATION AND MINDFULNESS

Meditation and mindfulness are powerful practices that help calm the mind, reduce stress, and increase awareness. They allow us to be fully present in the moment, letting go of distractions and finding peace within. By focusing on the breath, bodily sensations, or a specific thought, meditation guides us to quiet the noise of daily life and connect with our inner selves.

Mindfulness, the practice of being aware of our thoughts and feelings without judgment, can be incorporated into everyday activities, such as walking, eating, or even listening to others.
